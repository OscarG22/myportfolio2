Background

Hello, I'm Oscar Grajeda. I'm 24 years old, and I'm the oldest of three. I graduated from Dobson High School in 2014, afterwards I attended community college for four years and never got a degree. It was very difficult for me to find something I enjoyed and/or wanted to persue as a career so I gave up college and dedited to work until I found something worth my time and money.


Luckily I was introduced to coding in early 2020 by a friend. That led me to doing extensive research and that's how I found Univercity of Arizona's Coding Bootcamp. It has been challenging but also fun since day one (yes I said fun). To my surprise I ended up really liking the world of Web Development.


To Infinity and Beyond!

At the moment my class and I are starting our third month learning about Node.js. This bootcamp is six months long which is so much better than two or four years. I'm really looking forward to learning as much as I can to (hopefully) be employeed before or right after graduation. I think it would be really cool to work for Netflix.
